The Greatest Andrea Bocelli Duets Ever

Coley Reed
Updated March 13, 2020 633 votes 388 voters

The best Andrea Bocelli duets feature the top artists from all corners of the music industry. Since bursting into the public eye with his legendary Sarah Brightman duet, "Time to Say Goodbye," the Italian tenor has become, arguably, the most famous opera singer in modern history.

Since his breakthrough, Bocelli's skill and popularity have led some of the world's biggest singers to record songs alongside him, from veteran vocalists like Barbra Streisand and Tony Bennett, to modern pop superstars like Ariana Grande, Ed Sheeran, Dua Lipa, and Ellie Goulding. Naturally, the most renowned opera singers, like Luciano Pavarotti and Aida Garifullina, have joined forces in the studio and onstage with Bocelli over years. And, his talent clearly runs in the family, as was seen in "Fall On Me," his famous duet with son Matteo Bocelli.
